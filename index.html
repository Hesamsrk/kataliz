About Kataliz

Your club as you always dreamed!

Kataliz is a currency option for blockchain-backed products & services geared towards mainstream consumers. We want to elevate everyday experiences – fan engagement in entertainment, alternative payment solutions for conventional products, and more.

  • $KTZ – Is The Rocket Fuel for market.kataliz.ir
  • Fan Tokens bought on market.kataliz.ir allow users to influence their teams via popular vote and become eligible for engagement-based team rewards and recognition.
  • All Fan Tokens are minted using the Kataliz blockchain & all on-platform voting is executed as a series of smart contracts which run through the Kataliz blockchain.
